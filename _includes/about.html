How does it work?

Visualize running workouts from your Apple Watch on your iPhone, and export those workouts so you can upload them to your favorite online running community.

Run

Wear your Apple Watch, leave your iPhone at home. After calibrating your stride, your watch is surprisingly accurate in keeping track of your pace, heart rate, and cadence. Save your runs in the Activity app so they end up in the Health app on your iPhone.

Analyze

For each individual workout, the Workouts app can visualize heart rate, pace and cadence over time (with average, minimum and maximum values). You can also see the distance, duration, burned energy and total number of steps.

Share

Each workout can be saved to a .tcx file, which is a standard exchange format supported by most online running communities. This means you can export your workouts through iTunes and upload them for further analysis and social sharing.
