How does it work?

Visualize running or walking workouts from your Apple Watch on your iPhone, upload them to your favorite online running community, or share a summary on social media.

Run

Wear your Apple Watch, leave your iPhone at home. After calibrating your stride, your watch is surprisingly accurate in keeping track of your pace, heart rate, and cadence. Save your runs or walks in the Activity app on your watch so they end up in the Health app on your iPhone.

Analyze

For each individual workout, the Workouts app will visualize heart rate, pace and cadence over time (with average, minimum and maximum values). You can also see the distance, duration, burned energy and total number of steps. A handy list view allows to compare runs at a glance.

Share

Workouts can be exported (through iTunes, Dropbox, email) as .tcx files (a standard exchange format supported by most online running communities), so you can upload them for further analysis. Alternatively, you can quickly share a summary of your workout on social media.
